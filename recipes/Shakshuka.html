Shakshuka Recipe

A bold North African & Middle Eastern dish of poached eggs in a spicy tomato and pepper sauce, perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even dinner.

Ingridients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • paprika
  • Salt
  • 4-6 Eggs
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Heat Oil
  2. Add Garlic
  3. Stir in tomatoes, paprika, cumin, chili flakes, salt, and pepper.
  4. Make small wells in the sauce and gently crack eggs into each one
  5. Cover and cook for 5-7 minutes
  6. Garnish with parsley/cilantro and feta if using. Serve with warm bread or pita.
